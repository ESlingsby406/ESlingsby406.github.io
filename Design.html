Design

Design and technology is one of the more fun subjects I take and it involves us completing a number of projects throughout the years. These involve analysing products, making conclusions about why its a good design, and then going on and designing a product of our own to solve a real world problem.

Project 1 - Iconic Chair

This is my first project of Design A-level. In this project we each get assigned a famous chair and have to produce a scale model of it. The chair I was assigned was the Panton S chair, designed by Verner Panton in 1958 and is still is mass production today.
