Robotics

Me and 5 others take part in a seasonal Vex robotics competition where we have to build a robot to complete a specific task and score points against other teams. we first compete in a number of regional competitions for a chance to make it to the national competition where we then compete against teams from all over the country for a chance to make it to internationals where we face teams from all over the world.
